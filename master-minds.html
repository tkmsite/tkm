and Deeni Taleem (Fikeh/Akhbar)


MESSAGE FOR STUDENTS:
The purpose of education is not only to learn information but also to apply those in real life. This quiz show provides the platform to showcase your real world problem solving skills and assesses how you consume knowledge. It will play a major role in building up your thinking skills, time management skills, team work and etc.

We look forward to seeing you on the playing field.

MESSAGE FOR PARENTS:
This quiz show motivates your children and helps develop loads of soft skills along with a boost of confidence.
